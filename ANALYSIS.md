## üèÜ Top 5 News Websites in Gujarat (Rajkot)

1.  **Divya Bhaskar (divyabhaskar.co.in)**
    *   **Strength:** The market leader. Excellent mobile app, hyper-local news (My City), and a very modern, visual interface.
    *   **Key Feature:** "D-Live" (Live updates), E-Paper, and extensive video coverage.

2.  **Akila News (akilanews.com)**
    *   **Strength:** The "Heart of Rajkot". Known for its **Evening News** (Sanje Samachar). Extremely loyal readership in Saurashtra.

3.  **Sandesh (sandesh.com)**
    *   **Strength:** Strong legacy competitor to Bhaskar. Good balance of political and local news.
    *   **Key Feature:** "Sandesh City" sections and strong editorial columns.

4.  **TV9 Gujarati (tv9gujarati.com)**
    *   **Strength:** The leader in **Video & Live News**. People go here to *watch* news, not just read it.
    *   **Key Feature:** 24/7 Live TV streaming embedded directly on the home page.

5.  **VTV Gujarati (vtvgujarati.com)**
    *   **Strength:** Digital-first approach. Very active on social media and fast with breaking news notifications.
    *   **Key Feature:** "Web Stories" (Instagram-style news) and viral video content.
